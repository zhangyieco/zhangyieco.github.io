About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Social Security at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE, 上海财经大学). My research interests are health economics and industrial organization. My dissertation discusses the impact of China's long-term care insurance on the entry decisions of service providers.

You can contact me via email: zhangyieco at gmail dot com.
