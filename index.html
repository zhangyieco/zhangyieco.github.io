Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Social Security at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics (SUFE, 上海财经大学). My research interests are health economics and industrial organization. My dissertation examines how China’s long-term care insurance influences the entry decisions of service providers.

You can contact me via email: zhangyieco [at] gmail [dot] com.
